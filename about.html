About Us

Floral House is a family owned buisness, based in the heart of Calgary downtown. Rooted in a love for creativity we've spent 2 years bringing special moments alive. We offer high quality flowers and designs sourced locally from within canada and are dedicated to providing a personalized experience as well as quality service. We value building a long term relationship with our customers, treating everyone with care.Thank you for letting us be a part of your special moments!
